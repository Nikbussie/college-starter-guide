Your 7-Day Plan

  1. Day 1 — Activate Accounts

    Set up student email, LMS (e.g., Canvas), and portal access.

  2. Day 2 — Build Schedule

    Confirm registration; add class times, commute, meals, and breaks.

  3. Day 3 — Find Resources

    Locate advising, tutoring, library, and IT help desk hours.

  4. Day 4 — Study Routine

    Create a weekly study plan; choose a quiet study spot.

  5. Day 5 — Money Basics

    Review financial aid, textbooks options, and budget apps.

  6. Day 6 — Community

    Join a club or event; introduce yourself to classmates.

  7. Day 7 — Check-in

    Skim syllabi; plan your first week; set reminders.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many credits should I take?

Most freshmen start with 12–15 credits; confirm with your advisor.

When should I buy books?

After the first class—check the syllabus; some materials are optional or free.

What are office hours?

Times when instructors meet students—go early, bring questions, and follow up.
